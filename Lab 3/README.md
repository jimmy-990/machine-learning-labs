# Lab 3 - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

In this notebook I explore the Dirty Cafe Sales dataset which contains 10,000 cafe transactions and includes missing values and inconsistent entries to simulate real world data.
I begin by inspecting the structure of the dataset and converting the necessary columns to numeric format, then I examine missing values, duplicate records and unique categories to better understand the data quality.
After that I analyze the distribution of quantity and total spending and explore revenue across different items and payment methods.
I also study the relationship between price per unit and total spending and review correlations between numerical variables before analyzing the monthly revenue trend to observe overall sales behavior.

## Outcomes
- The analysis shows that total spending increases with higher quantity and higher price per unit.
- Revenue varies across different items with some products contributing more to overall sales.
- The correlation analysis confirms a strong relationship between quantity and total spent while other numerical relationships are relatively weak.
- Monthly revenue remains fairly stable with moderate fluctuations throughout the year.
